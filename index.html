Other perks worth using (no statement credit, but valuable)

Global Lounge Collection

1,550+ lounges: Centurion Lounges, Priority Pass Select (enroll), Delta Sky Club (10 visits/yr on Delta flights), Plaza Premium & partners. Add card to your mobile wallet / Amex app for entry.

Global Entry / TSA PreCheck

Up to $120 statement credit for the application fee, once every ~4 years. Pay the app fee with your Platinum and it auto-credits.

Fine Hotels + Resorts extras

Beyond the $600 credit: free breakfast, room upgrades, late checkout, and a ~$100 property credit per FHR stay. Stack with the hotel credit above.

Hotel elite status

Complimentary Hilton Honors Gold & Marriott Bonvoy Gold (enrollment required in the Amex Benefits section).

Rental car status

Hertz President's Circle, Avis Preferred, National Emerald Club Executive — enroll once via Amex.

Purchase & travel protections

Cell phone protection (pay your bill with the card), baggage insurance, trip delay/cancellation, extended warranty, return protection.

Amex Offers

Rotating add-on deals (spend $X get $Y). Open the Amex app → Offers and "add to card" the ones you'll use — separate from the credits above.

Membership Rewards earning

5x on flights booked direct/Amex Travel and on prepaid hotels via Amex Travel. Use the card for these to maximize points.
